Frequently Asked Questions

Can I write my problem in Hindi or Assamese? ▼
Yes! You can describe your problem in any language — Hindi, Assamese, or English. The AI understands all three and will write the output letter in whichever language you choose.
Is the letter legally valid? ▼
The letters follow standard formal complaint formats used in India. For serious legal matters, we recommend having a local advocate review the letter before sending. The RTI and Legal Notice formats follow standard Indian templates.
Where do I send the letter after downloading? ▼
Print the letter, sign it, and send via Registered Post (Speed Post) to keep a delivery record. For bank and telecom complaints, you can also email the PDF directly to their grievance email.
What is an RTI Application? ▼
RTI stands for Right to Information. Under the RTI Act 2005, any Indian citizen can request information from any government office. This is a powerful tool to get answers when offices don't respond to regular complaints.
Can I edit the letter after it's generated? ▼
Yes! Click the "Edit Letter" button below the preview to make manual changes directly in the letter. You can then download the edited version.
