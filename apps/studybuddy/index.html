Study Tips 💡

Smart habits for Class 9 & 10 students.

⏱️

Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes, rest for 5. After 4 cycles, take a 20-minute break. Your brain learns better in short bursts.

Productivity
✍️

Write to Remember

Don't just read — write summaries in your own words. For Maths, write out each step. For History, make timelines.

Memory
🔁

Spaced Repetition

Revise a topic after 1 day, then 3 days, then 7 days. This tricks your brain into storing info in long-term memory.

Revision
🎯

Understand, Don't Mug

For CBSE Class 9 & 10, understanding concepts earns more marks than memorising. Ask "why" before you ask "what".

Strategy
📐

Maths: Show Every Step

Even if you get the right answer, examiners give step marks. Practice writing every calculation clearly.

Maths
🌐

Social Science: Use Maps

For Geography and History, drawing rough maps and diagrams helps you remember locations, events, and connections.

Social Science
📖

English: Read Every Day

Read newspapers or short stories for 15 minutes daily. It improves comprehension, grammar, and writing speed naturally.

English
🔬

Science: Draw Diagrams

Label diagrams neatly — they carry easy marks. Practice drawing the human heart, plant cells, and circuit diagrams weekly.

Science
