# Assam Education System - Comprehensive Report for ExamCraft Integration

## Executive Summary

This report provides detailed information about all education boards operating in Assam, India, specifically for Classes 1-10. This data is intended to expand the ExamCraft AI Question Paper Generator app from its current scope (Classes 9-10) to cover the full spectrum of Classes 1-10 across all major boards.

---

## Part 1: Education Boards Operating in Assam

### 1.1 ASSEB (Assam State School Education Board) - PRIMARY STATE BOARD

**Full Name:** Assam State School Education Board (formed September 2024)

**Structure:**
- **Division I (formerly SEBA):** Classes 9-10 (Secondary)
- **Division II (formerly AHSEC):** Classes 11-12 (Higher Secondary)
- **SCERT Assam:** Governs Classes 1-8 (Elementary Education)

**Official Website:** http://www.asseb.in/

**Key Statistics (2024-25):**
- Total Government/Provincialized Schools: 44,300
  - Lower Primary Schools (Classes 1-5): 33,394
  - Upper Primary Schools (Classes 6-8): 6,343
  - Secondary/Sr. Secondary Schools (Classes 9-10): 4,563
- Enrollment: ~3.5 million students (Classes 1-8), ~670,000 (Classes 9-10)

**Medium of Instruction Available:**
- Assamese (majority)
- Bengali
- Bodo
- English
- Hindi
- Manipuri (Meitei)
- Hmar
- Nepali
- Mizo
- Khasi
- Garo
- Karbi
- Urdu

---

### 1.2 CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education)

**Nature:** National-level board

**Schools in Assam:**
- Kendriya Vidyalayas (KV)
- Jawahar Navodaya Vidyalayas (JNV)
- Private CBSE-affiliated schools (primarily in urban areas like Guwahati, Jorhat, Dibrugarh)

**Classes Covered:** Classes 1-12

**Medium:** Primarily English and Hindi

**Official Website:** https://www.cbse.gov.in/

---

### 1.3 ICSE/ISC (Council for the Indian School Certificate Examinations)

**Nature:** Private national board

**Schools in Assam:** Select private schools in urban areas (Guwahati, Dibrugarh, Silchar)

**Classes Covered:**
- ICSE: Classes 1-10
- ISC: Classes 11-12

**Medium:** English

**Official Website:** https://www.cisce.org/

---

### 1.4 AJBEC (Assam Jatiya Bidyalay Education Council)

**Nature:** Private state-level council managing "Jatiya Bidyalaya" schools.

**Schools in Assam:** Over 1,000 Jatiya Bidyalayas across every district, including rural areas like Nagaon (Kachua Tiniali), Morigaon, and Jorhat.

**Classes Covered:** Classes 1-12 (HSLC/HS via SEBA/AHSEC affiliation).

**Medium:** Primarily Assamese.

**Official Website:** https://assamjatiyabidyalay.com/

#### CLASSES 9-10 (SECONDARY / HSLC)

**Administrative Body:** ASSEB Division-I (formerly SEBA)

**Core Subjects (Compulsory):**
1. **First Language (MIL)** - Assamese/Bengali/Bodo/Hindi/Manipuri/Hmar/Nepali/Mizo/Khasi/Garo/Karbi/Urdu/English (IL)
2. **Second Language** - English
3. **General Science** - Physics, Chemistry, Biology combined
4. **General Mathematics** - Algebra, Geometry, Trigonometry, Statistics
5. **Social Science** - History, Geography, Political Science, Economics

**Elective/Vocational Subjects (Optional, choose any one):**
- Advanced Mathematics
- Computer Science/IT
- Home Science
- Commerce
- Fine Arts
- Music/Dance
- Garment Designing
- Weaving and Textile Design
- Wood Craft
- Agriculture
- Horticulture
- Yoga & Physical Education
- Various NSQF (National Skill Qualification Framework) vocational courses

**Examination Pattern:**
- **HSLC (High School Leaving Certificate)** at end of Class 10
- Theory: 90 marks + Internal Assessment: 10 marks = 100 marks per subject
- Pass marks: 30 out of 100
- Question distribution: 50% objective type, 50% subjective

### 2.4 AJBEC (Jatiya Bidyalay) Curriculum Structure

#### CLASSES 1-8 (AJB PRIMARY & MIDDLE)

**Administrative Body:** AJB Trust & Education Council (AJBEC).

**Key Features:**
- **Custom Textbooks:** Uses proprietary AJB Council textbooks for all subjects.
- **Science Practicals:** Mandatory laboratory work starting from Class 3.
- **Assam History:** Taught as a separate, mandatory subject from Classes 6-8.
- **Spoken English:** Regular daily classes for English communication from Class 5.
- **Evaluation:** Combination of weekly tests, terminal exams, and annual assessments.

---

#### CLASSES 9-10 (HSLC PREPARATION)

**Administrative Body:** Affiliated with **SEBA** for board exams, but follows AJBEC's pedagogical approach.

**Core Subjects:** Same as SEBA (English, MIL, Math, Science, Social Science), but often includes AJB's own advanced materials for internal preparation.

```javascript
BOARD -> classes -> Class N -> subjects -> Subject -> chapters
```

1. Add Classes 1-8 to each board

Each board needs specific rules:

**ASSEB/SCERT (Classes 1-8):**
- Follow SCERT textbook sequence
- Include Assamese cultural references
- Follow Gunotsav learning outcomes
- Age-appropriate language

**ASSEB/SEBA (Classes 9-10) - EXISTING:**
- HSLC exam pattern
- 50% objective questions
- Assam-specific context in Social Science/English

**CBSE (Classes 1-8):**
- Follow NCERT curriculum
- Activity-based questions
- CCE pattern for Classes 6-8

**CBSE (Classes 9-10) - EXISTING:**
- AISSE pattern
- Competency-based questions
- Case-based questions

**ICSE (Classes 1-8):**
- Detailed, comprehensive coverage
- English-heavy curriculum
- More emphasis on literature and grammar

**ICSE (Classes 9-10) - EXISTING:**
- Detailed sub-questions
- Internal assessment component
- English literature emphasis

**Classes 9-10:**
- Theory: 80 marks (CBSE) / 90 marks (SEBA)
