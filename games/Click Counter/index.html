How to Play

Click the "➕ Click Me!" button to add a random number to your current total.

Try to reach exactly the target number shown at the top.

If you go over the target, you lose!

Use the Hint button to see if your next move will win or lose.

Use Undo to take back your last move (once per game).

Select difficulty to change the range of numbers and target.
